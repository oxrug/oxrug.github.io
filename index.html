Oxford Ruby User Group

The Oxford Ruby User Group is a chance for local people using or interested in Ruby or Rails to get together in a friendly environment and chat. We try to meet on a monthly basis. Please feel free to join us!

Check the OxRUG meetup page to find out when we're next meeting.

If you've got any questions, want to chat about something or just can't wait until the next meetup chat to us on the #oxrug channel in the Digital Oxford Slack.
